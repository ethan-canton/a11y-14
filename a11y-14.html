How to make Raspberry & Chocolate Cake

Total Time To Make Cake:

Total Time: 1 hour 35 minutes.
Preparation Time: 35 minutes.

Cake Ingredients

  • Vegetable-oil cooking spray
  • 1.5 cups of all-purpose flour
  • One fourth cup Dutch-process cocoa powder
  • Three fourth teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of coarse salt
  • 1.5 sticks of unsalted butter. The butter should be room temperature
  • 1.25 cups of sugar
  • 3 large eggs. The eggs should be room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon raspberry liqueur, such as Chambord or framboise (optional)
  • 1 cup buttermilk. The buttermilk should be room temperature
  • 4 ounces of bittersweet chocolate (61 percent cacao), melted and cooled
Ingredients List







Filling Ingredients

  • 4 packages (6 ounces each) fresh raspberries (a scant 6 cups)
  • Three fourth cup plus 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • Pinch of coarse salt
  • 2 teaspoons of fresh lemon juice
  • Chocolate Frosting

Directions

Step 1

Cake: Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Lightly coat three 8 inch by 2 inch round cake pans with cooking spray.
Line bottoms of pans with parchment paper.
In a large bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
In another large bowl, beat butter with sugar on high speed until light and fluffy, 3 minutes.
Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
Beat in liqueur.
Reduce speed to low and add flour mix in 3 batches, alternating with buttermilk in two batches.
Beat to combine, scraping down sides of bowl as needed.
Beat in melted chocolate.

Step 2

Divide batter evenly among the prepared pans, smoothing tops with an offset spatula.
Bake, rotating pans halfway through front to back and top to bottom, until a tester inserted in centers comes out clean, about 25 minutes.
Let cakes cool in pans on a wire rack for 10 minutes.
Run a small sharp knife around edges to clean off excess cake, then invert cake onto rack.
Remove parchment paper and let it cool completely, wait for 25 minutes.
With a serrated knife, trim tops of cakes so they are level.

Step 3

Filling: While cakes are baking, stir together 3 cups raspberries, sugar, salt, and lemon juice in a medium saucepan.
Cook over high, stirring frequently and mashing with the back of a spoon, until mixture comes to a boil, 2 minutes.
Continue to cook and stir until mixture thickens and clings to the spoon.
Continue cooking and stirring for 7 more minutes.
You should have 1 and one third cups at this point.
Let cakes cool for 30 minutes.
Stir in 2 cups raspberries, reserving remainder.

Step 4

Spread half of filling over one cake.
Top with second layer; spread remaining filling over top.
Top with final layer, cut-side down.
Refrigerate cake and cover it, at least 1 hour and up to overnight.
Spread frosting over top the and sides of cake.
Decorate cake with remaining raspberries.
Now go ahead and enjoy the amazing cake!
